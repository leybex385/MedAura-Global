Our Leadership

Maya Miller

Senior Partner, MedAura Global

Maya Miller is the Senior Partner of MedAura Global, an international medical aesthetics equipment trading company specializing in global sourcing, healthcare technology distribution, and cross-border medical solutions.

She has over 16 years of experience in international business, healthcare trade, and global market development, with extensive expertise in North American and Asia-Pacific markets. Throughout her career, Maya has successfully built strategic partnerships between manufacturers, distributors, hospitals, and aesthetic clinics, helping healthcare organizations access advanced medical technologies through efficient international supply chains.

Today, Maya leads the company's international strategy, helping healthcare providers and aesthetic clinics gain access to innovative medical technologies through compliant global sourcing and distribution networks. Maya currently oversees a multidisciplinary international team of 15 professionals.

Philanthropy

Healthcare Without Borders Initiative (2022)

In 2022, Maya led MedAura Global's "Healthcare Without Borders Initiative," partnering with nonprofit organizations to donate patient monitoring devices and essential medical equipment to community clinics in Southeast Asia.

The initiative supplied digital blood pressure monitors, pulse oximeters, and portable oxygen concentrators to underserved healthcare facilities while also supporting clinical staff through equipment training and technical guidance.

To date, the program has benefited more than 2,000 patients by improving access to basic diagnostic and monitoring technologies in rural communities.
